Recommended Validation Strategy

Implementing a "Verify, then Trust" validation workflow is essential. A valid signature is merely the first step; it must be coupled with rigorous technical audits.

1

Signature Check

Verify if the X.509 certificate is mathematically valid and originates from an entity on the 2026 Global Trust List.

2

Revocation Check

Perform real-time checks to ensure the signer's certificate has not been compromised or revoked (addressing R9).

3

Temporal Anchor Check

Confirm that the manifest hash is anchored by an RFC 3161 Trusted Timestamp Authority (TSA) to prevent timeline spoofing.

4

Ingredient Graph Audit

Scan the additive history for "unsigned gaps" or "redacted parents" that indicate a break in the provenance chain.

5

Physical Reality Check (VtT Audit)

Do not blindly trust signatures. Cross-reference shadow vectors and Noise Pattern Analysis (NPA) uniformly against metadata.

Technical Audit Checklist

Forensic verification protocols for "Verify, then Trust" (VtT) assessment.

🔐 1. Foundational Integrity & Signature Audit

X.509 Certificate Validation

Verify leaf certificate originates from a certified CA on the 2026 C2PA Trust List (DigiCert, SSL.com, etc.).

Real-Time Revocation Check (OCSP)

Perform a live OCSP lookup to ensure signing hardware keys haven't been compromised or revoked.

Content Binding Verification

Recalculate bit-level SHA-256/384 hashes for assets to ensure zero-tolerance integrity for archival masters.

⏳ 2. Temporal & Freshness Audit

RFC 3161 TSA Token Check

Confirm manifest hash is anchored by an external Time-Stamp Authority.

Attestation Freshness Check (Nonces)

Verify presence of nonces in attestation tokens to prevent replayed sessions.

Cryptographic Chaining Consistency

Inspect relative ordering in VAP framework; ensure each entry includes the hash of the preceding manifest.

🌿 3. Ingredient Graph & Lineage Audit

Redaction Verification (V3 Spec)

Ensure hash-links to original ingredients remain verifiable even if metadata has been redacted.

"Unsigned Gap" Detection

Identify ingredients entering the pipeline without C2PA signatures and flag assets.

👁️ 4. Visual-Metadata Reality Audit (VtT)

Shadow Vector Analysis

Confirm geometric shadow convergence matches claimed metadata (GPS/Time).

Noise Pattern Analysis (NPA)

Verify stochastic noise signature (PRNU) is uniform across the entire frame.
